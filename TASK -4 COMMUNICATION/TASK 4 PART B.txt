SOCIAL MEDIA CHECK LIST FOR COLLEGE STUDENT

Do’s

Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Protect your privacy—use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your values and goals.
Be respectful in conversations—engage politely even in disagreements.


Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.).
Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
Don’t overshare details about your daily life or location.
Don’t believe or spread unverified rumors or fake news.
