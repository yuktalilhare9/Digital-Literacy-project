Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading attachments from unverified sources.
Keep your social media accounts private and limit personal information shared online.
Always verify the identity of people contacting you before sharing any information.
Do not use public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions like banking or logins.
Regularly update your apps and devices to protect against security vulnerabilities.
Be cautious while joining online groups, forums, or unknown communities.
Log out from accounts when using shared or public devices.

UPI / Financial Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone under any circumstances.
Do not approve unknown “collect requests” or scan random QR codes to receive money.
Verify payment requests carefully—receiving money never requires entering a PIN.

Reporting Cybercrime
Report incidents at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance
